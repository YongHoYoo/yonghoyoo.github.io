About Me

I am driven by the challenge of solving real-world problems through optimization, computational methods, and cross-domain integration, maximizing efficiency and impact. Rather than pursuing purely theoretical ideals, I focus on crafting solutions that can be deployed in practice—turning complex algorithms into actionable, high-value results.
At Koh Young Technology in San Diego, I have been captivated by finding the sweet spot where cutting-edge research meets tangible impact, from combinatorial optimization and computer vision to intelligent automation, understanding the world through cameras and sensors while integrating diverse data sources to guide practical decision-making.
